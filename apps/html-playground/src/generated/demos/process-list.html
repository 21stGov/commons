How to apply for a permit

  1. 1

    Gather your documents

    Collect proof of ownership, a site plan, and a government-issued ID before you start the application.
  2. 2

    Complete the application

    Fill out every required field on the permit form. You can save a draft and return to it later.
  3. 3

    Pay the filing fee

    Fees vary by permit type. You will see the exact amount before you submit payment.
  4. 4

    Await review

    A reviewer will contact you within 10 business days if anything else is needed.
